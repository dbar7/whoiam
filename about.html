About Me

Hello! My name is Diego, and this website is my personal space on the internet. I am passionate about technology, design, and exploring the web.

Here, you'll find a collection of my thoughts, projects, and things I find interesting. I was heavily inspired by the optimistic and clean aesthetic of the mid-2000s, and I wanted to recreate that feeling here.

About This Website

This website is a tribute to the Frutiger Aero design style, which was popular from roughly 2004 to 2013. It's known for its glossy textures, skeuomorphic details, and heavy use of nature imagery.

The goal was to create a corner of the web that feels bright, optimistic, and human-centric, as a contrast to the flat, minimalist design that is common today. Everything here, from the colors to the glassy buttons, was designed to evoke that specific feeling of early digital wonder.

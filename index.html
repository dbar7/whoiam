What is Frutiger Aero?

Frutiger Aero is a design style popular from the mid-2000s to early 2010s. It's characterized by:

  • Glassy, transparent interfaces
  • Glossy buttons and UI elements
  • Bright, optimistic colors (especially greens and blues)
  • Images of nature, water, and skies
  • A sense of depth and realism (skeuomorphism)
